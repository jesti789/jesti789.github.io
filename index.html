About Me

I am a senior at the University of Alabama in Huntsville. I enjoy building things from software to cabinets. I have experience as a carpenter which has shaped my work-ethic and view on quality design. I have had a passion for computers since I was 12 years old when I started writing small scripts and game mods. I know multiple programming languages and have implemented several types of applications. I enjoy graphics programming and making games. I have made both 2D and 3D applications using frameworks, game engines, and directly with OpenGL.

Outside of programming, I like to skateboard and build things in my garage. I am also an Eagle Scout.
